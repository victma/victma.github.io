What is Serial Groover ?

Serial Groover is a website (we're sure you got that already) that lets you play songs and videos one after another, automatically.

Ok, but why would I want to use it ?

Let's say you are at Bob's, streaming some nice music from his computer. Suddenly, you think about this other song, which is sooooo coool.

Don't stop the music! Let Bob enjoy his song, and put yours in the queue. It will patiently wait its turn, and start as soon as the previous one finishes.

Nice !
